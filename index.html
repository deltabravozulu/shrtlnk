shrtlnk

Creates short links for common websites.

http://bit.ly/shrt_lnk (https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/shrtlnk/nccahogoimgbhghcjmghidnnngigcagi)

e.g. amzn.com/B01A6G35IQ
